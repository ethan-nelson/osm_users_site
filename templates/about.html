About

This site uses the OSM Diff Tool on planet diff files to extract users who have made an edit and the date/time of that edit. If the user does not exist in my database, they will be added; otherwise, their most recent edit date is refreshed.

At the moment, I do not have information on users prior to the license change included. This is mostly the result of not spending time yet to ensure the CC-BY-SA diff files are compatible with my diff tool.
